Today, boys attend three locations – Wetherby Notting Hill, Wetherby Kensington and Wetherby Marylebone – which deliver outstanding pre-prep education. Children aged 4 to 8 learn inside two five-storey Victorian terraced townhouses, easily recognised thanks to the distinctive red doors that are synonymous with Wetherby School. For nearly half a century, generations of young boys have passed through the Wetherby doors wearing the neat grey-and-crimson uniform, the appearance of which has remained largely unchanged for many years.</p>
