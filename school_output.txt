How they teach
Wetherby provides an environment that promotes educational excellence and tradition through a forward-thinking, holistic curriculum, which meets the needs of individual pupils. With our individualised learning strategies, each boy learns to challenge himself, pursue high standards, and seek accomplishment in all he does so that his talents shine through. Today, boys attend three locations – Wetherby Notting Hill, Wetherby Kensington and Wetherby Marylebone – which deliver outstanding pre-prep education. Children aged 4 to 8 learn inside two five-storey Victorian terraced townhouses, easily recognised thanks to the distinctive red doors that are synonymous with Wetherby School. For nearly half a century, generations of young boys have passed through the Wetherby doors wearing the neat grey-and-crimson uniform, the appearance of which has remained largely unchanged for many years.
