Located in Paris, it offers a range of preparation courses including Prépa Médecine, Prépa Sciences Po, engineering and business school entrance exams both post-Bac and post-preparatory, and specific subject stages for high school students. The institution provides courses in-person at its centers in prestigious Parisian schools like Lycée Fénelon Sainte-Marie and Stanislas, as well as online for broader accessibility. With nearly two decades of experience, it prides itself on equipping students with methodologies, high-level courses, and comprehensive understanding necessary to excel in their educational pursuits.</p></div>
