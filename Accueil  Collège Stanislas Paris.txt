<div class="wp-block-columns"><div class="wp-block-column" style="flex-basis:66.66%"><h2 class="wp-block-heading">About <em>Accueil - Collège Stanislas Paris</em></h2><p>Collège Stanislas Paris, founded in 1804, is a prestigious private Catholic institution located in the heart of Paris. The school offers education from primary to higher secondary levels, including well-regarded 'classes préparatoires,' which prepare students for entry into the top-tier French higher education institutions. Stanislas is known for its strong emphasis on a holistic education model that combines rigorous academics with personal development, spiritual growth, and social responsibility. The school provides state-of-the-art facilities, including science laboratories, sports complexes, and comprehensive boarding options to accommodate students from across France. Renowned for producing well-rounded individuals, Stanislas students are encouraged to pursue excellence in both academic and extracurricular activities, fostering a community where talent and character are harmoniously developed.</p></div>

<div class="wp-block-column"><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Philosophy</h5><h3 class="wp-block-heading">How they&nbsp;<em>teach</em></h3><p>Stanislas draws on its Catholic roots and the essence of the Gospel to shape its educational philosophy, focusing on nurturing each student's unique talents and guiding them towards personal and academic excellence. The school prioritizes the development of character alongside intellectual growth, ensuring that all students become responsible, well-rounded adults capable of discerning their vocations and serving society. By fostering an environment of dedication, collaboration, and community, Stanislas invites students to actively engage in their learning and growth processes, embracing both the individual and collective aspects of education.</p></div>
<div class="wp-block-column"><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Outcomes</h5><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Where students&nbsp;<em>go</em></h3><p>Graduates of Stanislas consistently proceed to some of the most prestigious universities and higher education institutions both in France and internationally. The school's robust 'classes préparatoires' program is particularly successful in securing admissions to top engineering and business schools such as École Polytechnique and HEC Paris. With a focus on holistic education, students are well-equipped to embark on diverse career paths, excelling in fields ranging from science and technology to humanities and social sciences. Such success is a testament to the school's commitment to fostering academic excellence and personal integrity.</p></div>
