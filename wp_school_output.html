About Harrow School

Harrow School is one of the world’s most famous schools. Founded in 1572 under a Royal Charter granted by Elizabeth I, it is located on a 324-acre estate in Northwest London.

Philosophy

How they teach

The Harrow philosophy is based on the four values of Courage, Honour, Humility, and Fellowship. We teach students to lead and to serve.

Outcomes

Where students go

Harrow boys go on to leading universities worldwide, including Oxford, Cambridge, and Ivy League institutions.
